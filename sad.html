In the darkest of nights,
Where love's flame no longer ignites,
I wander lost in memories,
Of what once brought me to my knees.

A heart once whole, now torn apart,
Shattered dreams and a broken heart,
Each beat echoes with sorrow's cry,
As love's sweet promise fades and dies.

I search for solace in the rain,
Hoping it will wash away the pain,
But the tears that fall cannot replace,
The love I lost, the empty space.

And so I carry this heavy load,
A broken heart, a weary soul,
But I'll find strength to heal and mend,
And someday, love's light will shine again.
